Replacing an ERP is tempting because the pain feels systemic. But many operational bottlenecks come from missing visibility around a few high-value workflows.

## Start with the decision gap

Ask what leaders and supervisors cannot see quickly enough. Inventory exceptions, production delays, quality holds, and material shortages are often better starting points than a full platform replacement.

## Build around adoption

The best first system is usually the one teams can adopt without rewriting how they work overnight. Focused tools can produce measurable value while preserving room for deeper integration later.
