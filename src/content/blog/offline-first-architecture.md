Field teams work in factories, basements, rural sites, and busy construction zones. Connectivity is not guaranteed, so the product should be designed around continuity.

## Local-first workflows

Critical actions should work locally, queue safely, and sync predictably. The user should know what is saved, what is pending, and what needs attention.

## Conflict design matters

Offline systems are not only a backend concern. Product design must decide which fields can conflict, who resolves them, and how much history the team needs to trust the result.
