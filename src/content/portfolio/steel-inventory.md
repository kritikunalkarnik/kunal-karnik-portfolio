## Challenge

The plant team was tracking incoming material, shop-floor movement, and stock reconciliation across disconnected spreadsheets. Leadership did not have a dependable view of available inventory until the end of the day.

## Approach

Discovery mapped the actual material flow from gate entry through issue, return, and production usage. The product focused on the highest-friction moments: scanning, approval, exception handling, and supervisor visibility.

## Outcome

The new workflow reduced stock reconciliation time, improved supervisor confidence, and created a clean foundation for future ERP integration.
