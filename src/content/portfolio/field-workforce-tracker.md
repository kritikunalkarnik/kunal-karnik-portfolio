## Challenge

Project managers were relying on daily messages, manual photos, and spreadsheet summaries to understand field progress. Small reporting delays became planning errors.

## Approach

The solution standardized daily updates, connected evidence capture to project milestones, and surfaced exceptions before they became schedule risks.

## Outcome

Managers could review progress across sites in one dashboard, while field teams had a simpler way to submit structured updates.
