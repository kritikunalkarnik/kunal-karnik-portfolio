## Challenge

Shipment data lived across vendor sheets, warehouse tools, and email updates. Leaders knew something was delayed only after teams escalated manually.

## Approach

The dashboard consolidated status data, highlighted exceptions, and provided simple drill-down paths for vendor, warehouse, and shipment-level decisions.

## Outcome

The team moved from reactive escalation to proactive coordination, shortening the time between an exception and a corrective action.
